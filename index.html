ICover Smart Recovery Wrap

A smart recovery wrap designed to support athletes, injured patients, physical therapy users, and rehabilitation centers.

Product Overview

ICover is a wearable recovery wrap that combines heat therapy, cold therapy, massage, smart sensors, and app tracking into one portable device.

Smart Support

Helps users manage soreness, swelling, and stiffness.

Multi-Therapy

Combines heat, cold, and massage in one wrap.

App Tracking

Tracks recovery sessions and progress over time.

Who ICover Is For

Athletes

Use ICover after training, games, or physical strain.

Injured Patients

Support recovery from soreness, swelling, or stiffness.

Physical Therapy Users

Continue guided recovery routines at home.

Rehab Centers

Offer a smart recovery tool for client support.

Core Features

Heat Therapy

Supports muscle relaxation and stiffness relief.

Cold Therapy

Helps reduce swelling and discomfort.

Massage Mode

Provides comfort and circulation support.

Smart Sensors

Monitor temperature and usage time.

App Connection

Tracks routines and recovery progress.
